How to export

Easiest: Mac — Cmd+Shift+4, drag a box around the card. Windows — Win+Shift+S, drag a box.

Browser DevTools (sharper): Open DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Opt+I), right-click the card’s outer <article>, choose Capture node screenshot.

In Canvas: Course Settings → Course Card Image → upload your saved file.
